Goudy 150
Magazine Publishing Project
Goudy 150 consists in a exhibition, that will be promoted by the Cary Library in 2015, celebrating the anniversary of the type designer Frederic W. Goudy. As part of the assignment, our group should create the printed catalogue for the exhibition, and also the Awards section of his digital publication (for Ipad).

Project partners:
Carmem de Andrade, Allison Dougherty and Matthew Seemann
